MGuest WiFi Network

Our guest network provides basic internet access for the public good. Enter your email address and accept the terms of use to connect.

Are you a member of the U-M community? Configure your device to use MWireless for full network access.

Terms of Use

MGuest is a Wi-Fi network for University of Michigan guests. This network is allotted a limited share of network resources, does not provide encryption, is treated as an external network, and is available on a best effort basis. Certain U-M resources, such as library journals, are not accessible from this network.

MGuest is a part of the U-M IT environment. By using this resource, you agree to abide by the highest standards of responsibility. You are required to comply with all University policies, state, and federal laws concerning appropriate use of information technology. Non-compliance is considered a serious breach of community standards and may result in disciplinary or legal action.

For U-M students, faculty, staff and sponsored guests, MWireless and eduroam deliver better performance, allows mobile devices to stay connected, provides encryption, and allows access to all U-M resources.

For assistance, please contact 4-HELP.
